## Full Description

### What is SkillGPS?

SkillGPS is an intelligent career guidance and learning platform designed to help students, professionals, and career switchers navigate their career journey with confidence. By combining AI-powered assessments, resume analysis, and personalized learning paths, SkillGPS eliminates the guesswork from career planning.

### How It Works

1. **Take a Smart Assessment** - Complete a 4-step survey covering your interests, work style preferences, career goals, and confidence levels.

2. **Get AI-Powered Recommendations** - Our intelligent algorithm analyzes your responses against 50+ career paths and provides personalized career matches with confidence scores.

3. **Upload Your Resume** - Our advanced parser extracts your skills and compares them against your recommended career requirements, identifying exactly what skills you need to acquire.

4. **Follow Your Personalized Learning Path** - Access 200+ curated courses (free & paid) organized by skill level, with direct links to top learning platforms like Coursera, Udemy, and freeCodeCamp.

5. **Practice & Track Progress** - Use our AI chatbot to practice interview questions with NLP-based evaluation, and track your learning journey in real-time.
